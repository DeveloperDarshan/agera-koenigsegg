Koenigsegg

The Agera S had all the features and functions of the Agera R, but was created specifically for markets where E85 fuel was unavailable, which rendered the power-enhancing flex-fuel function of Agera R meaningless. Despite not running on high-octane biofuel, Koenigsegg still managed to extract 1100 Nm of torque and 1030 hp from the 5-liter bi-turbo V8 when running on 98 octane RON or 93 octane DIN.
